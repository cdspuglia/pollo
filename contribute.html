Thanks!

If you are reading this page...great! You're our hero! We really appreciate contributions from anyone and we are looking forward to accept your pull requests.

How to contribute

For quick bug fixing and other suggestions, just use the repository issue tracker. If you want to actually work on some features, instead, we suggest you to take a look at the development guidelines first, then browse the backlog to see what you can do and who you have to collaborate with, if working on a same feature.

I have some code ready to be merged. What now?

Great! Just open a pull request in the Pollo repository and wait for some of our core team to review your code. We could ask you to squash your commits in a unique one before merging, as we try to keep git history as clean as possible.

I still have doubts on what to do or how to do things...

No problems, just drop a message in our LinkedIn group, we will reply to you in no time (we know, it's an Italian group, but feel free to use English, of course).
